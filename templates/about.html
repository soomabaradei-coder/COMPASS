01  Overview

Beyond Digital Transformation.
Toward Intelligent Academic Services.

COMPASS transforms academic services through artificial intelligence — moving beyond digitized processes to intelligent automation, personalized student support, and data-driven academic decision-making.

02  Objectives

What the platform is built to do.

01
Streamline workflows. Simplify advising and approval processes end to end.
02
Go paperless. Eliminate paper forms and manual correspondence.
03
Automate compliance. Validate data and enforce ABET accreditation standards.
04
Empower with data. Give advisors and students real-time academic insight.
